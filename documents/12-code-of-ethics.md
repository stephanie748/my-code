# Code of Ethics

**Recovery Residence:** {{HOUSE_NAME}}
**Effective Date:** {{EFFECTIVE_DATE}}

Lifeline Medical Group Inc. and all staff at {{HOUSE_NAME}} adhere to the PARR Code of Ethics and the NARR Standard. Staff and management agree to:

1. **Integrity** — Operate honestly and transparently in all dealings with residents, families, referral sources, landlords, and regulators (DDAP, PARR/NARR).
2. **Resident Dignity** — Treat every resident with dignity, respect, and fairness regardless of race, sex, disability, religion, national origin, or funding source.
3. **Boundaries** — Maintain appropriate professional boundaries between staff and residents; no financial, romantic, or exploitative relationships with current residents.
4. **Confidentiality** — Protect resident confidentiality consistent with applicable law, disclosing information only as authorized or required.
5. **Non-Discrimination in Admission** — Apply admission and discharge criteria consistently and without discrimination.
6. **Safety First** — Prioritize resident and community safety in all housing and operational decisions.
7. **Financial Transparency** — Disclose all fees and financial terms in writing before move-in; no hidden charges.
8. **Conflict of Interest** — Disclose any conflict of interest (e.g., referral relationships, ownership interests) that could affect resident placement or care.
9. **Continuous Compliance** — Maintain current DDAP licensure, local rental licensure, zoning compliance, and PARR certification at all times; do not operate a house before licensing/zoning is confirmed for that address.
10. **Reporting** — Staff are required to report suspected abuse, exploitation, or rights violations to management and, where required, to the appropriate authority (DDAP, PARR, or law enforcement).
11. **Grievance Rights** — Uphold every resident's right to file a grievance without retaliation, per the Grievance Procedure.
