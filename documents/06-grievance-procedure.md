# Grievance Procedure

**Recovery Residence:** {{HOUSE_NAME}}
**Effective Date:** {{EFFECTIVE_DATE}}

## 1. Purpose

This procedure gives residents a clear, non-retaliatory path to raise concerns about house conditions, staff conduct, or decisions (including discharge decisions eligible for appeal).

## 2. Step 1 — Informal Resolution

Resident raises the concern directly with the House Manager, {{HOUSE_MANAGER_NAME}}, verbally or in writing. House Manager responds within {{STEP1_RESPONSE_WINDOW}}.

## 3. Step 2 — Written Grievance

If unresolved, resident submits a written grievance to Lifeline Medical Group Inc. (info@lifelinexo.com or 610-871-1547) within {{STEP2_FILING_WINDOW}} of the incident. The Executive Director, {{EXECUTIVE_DIRECTOR_NAME}}, reviews and responds in writing within {{STEP2_RESPONSE_WINDOW}}.

## 4. Step 3 — External Appeal

If still unresolved, or if the grievance concerns the Operator itself, the resident may contact:

- PARR: 215-380-0904, f.way@parronline.org
- DDAP: 717-783-8675

## 5. Non-Retaliation

No resident will be discharged, threatened, or otherwise retaliated against for filing a good-faith grievance.

## 6. Recordkeeping

All grievances, responses, and resolutions are logged and retained for {{RECORD_RETENTION_PERIOD}} for PARR inspection purposes.
