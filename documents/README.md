# PARR Documents

The 12 documents required for PARR certification, drafted on Lifeline Medical Group Inc. letterhead. Each is a Markdown template with `{{placeholders}}` for house-specific details.

Before submitting to PARR:

1. Fill in every blank (rent, fees, house manager name, curfew, dates).
2. Verify your insurance coverage amount with your agent — do not guess (see `07-insurance-record.md`).
3. Remember: DDAP licensing is separate from PARR certification, and often required alongside it.
